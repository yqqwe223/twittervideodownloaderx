❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does it say "Video cannot be parsed" or "Invalid link"?
A: Possible reasons: 1) Incorrect link format—ensure it's the FC2 video playback page URL; 2) Video has been deleted or set to private; 3) Content requires login to view; 4) Region restrictions block access. Please verify that the link plays normally in your browser.
Q: What should I do if download speed is slow?
A: Download speed is mainly limited by your network bandwidth and FC2’s servers. Suggestions: 1) Download during off-peak hours; 2) Try a different resolution; 3) Check your network stability; 4) Close other bandwidth-consuming applications.
Q: Can I download paid videos?
A: No. This tool only supports downloading publicly available free content. It cannot bypass paywalls for paid, rented, or subscription-based videos. Please respect content creators and the platform’s payment system.
Q: The downloaded video has no audio?
A: Normally, downloaded videos include audio. If there’s no sound, it might be because: 1) The original video has no audio; 2) Your browser has audio decoding issues; 3) Your media player doesn’t support the audio codec. Try playing it with third-party players like VLC or PotPlayer.
Q: What format are the downloaded files in?
A: Videos are downloaded in MP4 format (H.264 video codec + AAC audio codec)—the most widely compatible format, playable on all devices, operating systems, and media players.
Q: Does it support batch downloading or entire playlists?
A: Currently, the tool mainly supports single-video downloads. You can open multiple browser tabs with the downloader to download several videos simultaneously. Batch download functionality is under development—stay tuned!
